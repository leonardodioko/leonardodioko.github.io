Our business

Dioko & Sons Heritage Holdings Limited is a privately owned, closed-end, investment company incorporated in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region. We are a centralized repository and private holding vehicle for our shareholders' proprietary capital. We do not manage funds or portfolios for external clients or third parties. We do not provide investment advice.

Our mission is the long-term preservation and appreciation of capital through the strategic, diversified, balanced, and conservative deployment of capital in global financial markets and instruments. Our capital management philosophy is strictly focused on the long-term, eschewing high-frequency trading, speculative derivatives, and active commercial trading. When and where appropriate, the firm also examines opportunities for the philantropic and charitable distribution of its capital.
